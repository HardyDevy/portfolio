Trophies

Manchester United is one of the most successful clubs in Europe in terms of trophies won. They have won a record 20 top-division titles, including:

  • 13 Premier League titles (Most of any club)
  • 12 FA Cup titles
  • 21 FA Community Shield titles
  • 3 European Cup / Champions League titles
  • And many more...

Players

Manchester United has been the destination for numerous of the worlds best footballers. The likes of Cristiano Ronaldo, David Beckham, Eric Cantona, and many others have worn the badge of the red devils.

Fanbase

Manchester United has accumulated the largest fanbase amongst any football club in the world!
