Capstone Records

In my senior capstone project at Clarkson University, I crafted branding for a fictional record label and three bands. I designed logos, business cards, posters, and album covers, before bringing these designs to life in physical form.

Those Who Care's branding exudes a lively and fun-loving atmosphere with bright saturated colors, reflecting their energetic spirit. Their logo features playful fonts, creating an inviting and youthful visual identity that perfectly complements their music's joyful essence.

Rearview's branding captures the mesmerizing essence of gazing into a rearview mirror, using bright oranges and saturated colors to create a dreamlike atmosphere, complemented by an artful fusion of sleek, edgy fonts that mirror the band's introspective lyrics and hypnotic melodies.

Sounds of Daylight's branding embraces a serene ambiance, using rich purples and moody blues to create a dreamlike atmosphere. The color palette evokes a sense of calm and introspection, complemented by elegant fonts that give a touch of sophistication to their visual identity.
